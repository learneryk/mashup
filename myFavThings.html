Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter developed by Riot Games and released in 2020. The game combines precise gunplay with unique character abilities, creating a blend of traditional tactical shooters and hero-based gameplay. Players compete in 5-versus-5 matches where one team attacks and plants a device called the Spike, while the other team defends and attempts to stop them.

Each playable character, known as an Agent, has special abilities that influence strategy, teamwork, and map control. Because of its competitive ranking system, esports scene, and constant updates, Valorant has become one of the most popular tactical shooters worldwide. Success in the game depends on communication, aim accuracy, and smart use of abilities rather than pure reflex alone.
